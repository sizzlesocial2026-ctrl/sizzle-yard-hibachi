FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

How much does private hibachi cost?

Our classic birthday hibachi party starts at $60/person with a 10 guest minimum and $600 minimum total. Kids are $30/person. Sales tax is not included. Final sales tax is calculated based on the event address and the applicable CDTFA rate on the event date.

Do you offer mobile hibachi near me?

Sizzle Yard Hibachi serves many Southern California areas, including Los Angeles, Pasadena, Arcadia, San Gabriel, Alhambra, Monterey Park, Glendale, Burbank, Temple City, Rosemead, Orange County, and nearby cities. Send your city or ZIP code so we can confirm availability and travel.

Is this hibachi catering or hibachi at home?

This is hibachi at home with a mobile private chef experience. We bring the grill and cook on site, instead of simply dropping off catering trays.

Why are some hibachi companies cheaper?

A lower headline price may not include the same package details, guest minimum, setup help, menu choices, travel, tax handling, or party entertainment. We focus on a clear mobile hibachi package so hosts know what is included before paying a deposit.

How are food portions handled?

We use a standardized birthday hibachi package with clear adult and kid pricing, fried rice, appetizers, proteins, and optional upgrades. Final food quantities are based on confirmed guest count, menu choices, and upgrades before the event.

Do you use authorized event photos?

We choose Sizzle Yard-safe photos that match the party experience and avoid sensitive details like private addresses, license plates, clear child closeups, or images with another company's branding.

What is included?

Chef, food, hibachi grill, setup, cleanup, fried rice, appetizers, 2 proteins, and interactive entertainment are included. A small, controlled fire moment may be included when the setup is safe and allowed by the location.

Do you bring the grill?

Yes. We bring the hibachi grill and cooking setup to your location.

Can you do smaller parties?

Yes, but the minimum charge is $600.

What do we need to prepare?

Please have an outdoor cooking space ready. Plates, utensils, napkins, and cups are usually prepared by the host unless we confirm otherwise. Airbnbs, parks, and private venues may require approval for outdoor cooking, propane, grill setup, and fire effects.

Do you provide tables and chairs?

Tables and chairs may be available upon request. Availability depends on guest count, event location, and setup needs. We will confirm before you reserve.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Many nearby Southern California bookings can be served without a travel fee. Send your city or ZIP code and we will confirm before you reserve.

Can you do Airbnb, parks, or venues?

Often yes, but Airbnbs, parks, and private venues may require approval for outdoor cooking, propane, grill setup, and fire effects. The host is responsible for confirming location rules before booking.

What about rain or bad weather?

We need a safe outdoor cooking area. If weather may affect your event, contact us early so we can review the setup and options.

How do I reserve my date?

A $100 deposit is required only after we confirm availability and event details. Deposit and cancellation terms will be confirmed in writing before payment. Events cancelled within 36 hours of the start time may lose the deposit. One reschedule may be allowed based on availability.

Can you handle allergies?

Please tell us about any food allergies or dietary restrictions before booking. We can review requests and take reasonable precautions, but because we use shared grills, utensils, sauces, and prep areas, we cannot guarantee an allergen-free environment.

Is gratuity required?

Gratuity is optional and never required. Any tip is fully at the customer's discretion.
