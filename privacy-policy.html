Sizzle Yard Hibachi Privacy Policy

This page explains how we use information submitted through this website, phone calls, text messages, and booking requests.

Information We Collect

We may collect your name, phone number, event date, city or ZIP code, guest count, preferred start time, notes, menu requests, allergy information, venue rules, and messages you send to us.

How We Use Information

We use your information to respond to quote requests, confirm availability, estimate pricing, calculate sales tax, review setup needs, coordinate the event, and send booking-related messages.

Text Messages and Calls

When you submit a quote request, call us, or text us, we may respond by call or text about your request. We do not use your quote request as permission for unrelated automated marketing texts. You can ask us to stop texting you.

Payments

Deposit payments may be handled through a third-party payment provider such as Stripe. We do not store full card numbers on this website.

Analytics and Advertising

This website may use analytics or advertising tools to understand site visits, improve advertising, and measure quote requests. These tools may use cookies or similar technologies.

Sharing Information

We do not sell your booking information. We may share information with service providers who help us operate the website, process payments, communicate with customers, or provide the event service.

Photos and Videos

We may ask permission before using identifiable customer photos or videos for marketing. If you want a photo or video removed from our marketing, contact us.

Contact

To ask about your information, update a booking request, or request deletion of information you sent us, call or text Sizzle Yard Hibachi at 626-681-5258.
