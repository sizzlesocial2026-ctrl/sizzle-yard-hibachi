FAQ

Quick answers before you book.

How much does it cost?

Our classic birthday hibachi party starts at $60/person with a 10 guest minimum and $600 minimum total. Kids are $30/person.

What is included?

Chef, food, hibachi grill, setup, cleanup, fried rice, appetizers, 2 proteins, fire show, and interactive entertainment are included.

Do you bring the grill?

Yes. We bring the hibachi grill and cooking setup to your location.

Can you do smaller parties?

Yes, but the minimum charge is $600.

What do we need to prepare?

Please have an outdoor cooking space ready. Plates, utensils, napkins, and cups are usually prepared by the host unless we confirm otherwise.

Do you provide tables and chairs?

Tables and chairs may be available upon request. Tell us your guest count and we will confirm what we can provide.

Do you charge a travel fee?

Many nearby Southern California bookings can be served without a travel fee. Send your city or ZIP code and we will confirm before you reserve.

Can you do Airbnb, parks, or venues?

Often yes, but the location must allow outdoor cooking, propane, and a safe grill setup. Please confirm venue rules before booking.

What about rain or bad weather?

We need a safe outdoor cooking area. If weather may affect your event, contact us early so we can review the setup and options.

How do I reserve my date?

Send your date, city, guest count, and preferred start time. After we confirm availability, a $100 deposit holds your date.

Can you handle allergies?

Tell us about allergies before booking. We will review the request, but serious allergies may require special confirmation before we accept the event.
